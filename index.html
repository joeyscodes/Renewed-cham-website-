Our Story

Fresh from the Sea, Served with Soul

Cham brings the best of Cape Verdean cuisine to Santa Maria. Our chefs work directly with local fishermen to bring you the freshest catch daily. From grilled lobster to traditional catchupa, every dish tells a story of our islands. We combine indigenous recipes with modern techniques for an experience you won't forget.

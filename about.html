Our Heritage

Family, sea, and tradition since 2008

Since 2008

Cham started as a small family-run eatery on the shores of Santa Maria. What began with just six tables and a passion for fresh seafood has grown into a destination for travelers and locals alike, known for authentic Cape Verdean hospitality and innovative seafood dishes.

We work directly with local fishermen, farmers, and artisans to bring you the freshest ingredients. Every morning, our team visits the Santa Maria fish market to hand-select the catch of the day. Our kitchen is open, our hearts are warm, and our welcome is genuine.

Come share a meal with us – you'll leave as family.
